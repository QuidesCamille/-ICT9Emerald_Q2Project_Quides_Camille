Top Best Tourists Spots in the Philippines

The Philippines

The Philippines, an enchanting archipelago in Southeast Asia, is renowned for its diverse landscapes, vibrant cities, and warm-hearted people. As you plan your trip to this tropical paradise, consider exploring a myriad of captivating destinations. From majestic mountains to bustling urban centers, and the rich tapestry of its people, the Philippines promises a truly memorable experience as you run through your journey!



Rice Terraces

Rice Terraces located in Banaue, Ifugao, Philippines, were carved by the ancestors of the Igorot people. This is often reffered to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World"

Fort Santiago

Fort Santiago, located in Intramuros, Manila, Philippines, is an old settlement which is used as a defensive fortress during the Spanish colonial period.

Mayon Volcano

The Mayon Volcano, located in the province of Albay in Bicol. It is known for its perfectly conial shape. However, it is one of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines.

Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills is found in the state of Bohol, famous for its chocolate-like appearance which is the reason for why it is called "Chocolate Hills"
